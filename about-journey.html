Learn About Our Journey

2020 – Launched Online Legal Helpdesk

In 2020, we launched our 24/7 online legal helpdesk to assist clients during the pandemic, offering consultations via phone and video calls.

2019 – Expanded to Cyber & Family Law

We extended our legal services to include Cyber Law and Family Law, helping clients deal with online frauds and personal legal matters.

2018 – Opened Second Branch in Delhi

Due to increasing client demand, we opened our second office in Delhi to cater to civil and criminal litigation more efficiently.

2017 – Introduced Legal Awareness Workshops

We started organizing free legal awareness workshops in colleges and communities to educate people about their rights and legal remedies.

2016 – Achieved 100+ Successful Cases

This year marked a major milestone as we successfully handled over 100 civil and criminal cases across various courts.

2015 – Our Foundation Year

We began our journey in 2015 with a small team of passionate lawyers committed to justice, starting with civil litigation and property disputes.
